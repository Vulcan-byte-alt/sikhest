About Sikhest Performance

Founded by first-generation Punjabi brothers in Stockton, California, Sikhest Performance was born from a passion for automotive excellence and a commitment to quality. Our journey began with a simple goal: to provide premium automotive products at fair prices with exceptional service.

Today, we're proud to offer nationwide shipping on our extensive collection of tires, wheels, lift kits, and performance parts. Our team combines industry expertise with a dedication to customer satisfaction, ensuring every purchase enhances your vehicle's performance and appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, installation, and more. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team for personalized assistance.

We offer nationwide shipping on all our products. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days after your order has been processed.

For heavy items like wheels and lift kits, we use freight shipping which may take 5-7 business days. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order has shipped.

For customers in California, we also offer expedited 1-2 day shipping options at checkout.

Our website features a vehicle fitment tool that allows you to enter your vehicle's year, make, model, and trim to see compatible products.

For tires and wheels, you can also search by size if you know your current specifications.

If you're unsure about compatibility, our team of experts is available to help. Contact us with your vehicle details, and we'll assist you in finding the perfect fit.

For customers in the Stockton, CA area, we offer full installation services at our facility. You can book an installation appointment during checkout.

For customers outside our local area, we have a network of recommended installers across the country. Contact us for referrals in your area.

We also provide detailed installation guides for DIY installations, and our customer service team is available to assist with any questions during the process.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in new, unused condition with original packaging. Custom or special-order items may have different return terms.

All products come with the manufacturer's warranty, which varies by product. Warranty information is available on each product page.

In addition, we offer our Sikhest Performance Protection Plan for select products, which extends coverage beyond the manufacturer's warranty. Details are available during checkout.

Yes, we offer several financing options to make your purchase more affordable. During checkout, you can select from payment plans through our partners.

We offer 0% interest if paid in full within 6 months for orders over $500.

For commercial customers, we also offer net-30 terms with approved credit. Contact our business services team for more information.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

For orders with multiple items, they may ship separately depending on availability and shipping methods.

If you haven't received tracking information within 2 business days of your order confirmation, please contact our customer service team.
